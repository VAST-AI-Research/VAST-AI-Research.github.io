Latent Color Field Generation Framework

Image-conditioned Texture Generation Comparison

Occlusion handling

LaFiTe’s 3D-native formulation generates complete and coherent textures even in highly occluded regions where projection methods fail.

Application

Material Transfer

Direct material transfer with our method, where a 2D material image is used as the image condition to synthesize seamless PBR textures on the mesh.

Partial refinement

Local texture refinement with LaFiTe, resulting in a much higher level of local details.

Inpainting

LaFiTe flexibly combines inpainting with other multi-view generation approaches to achieve better results.
